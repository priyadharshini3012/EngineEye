Project Vision

EngineEye was developed as part of the Google Solution Challenge to solve a critical problem in the automotive industry: unpredictable mechanical failures in two-wheelers. By leveraging real-time IoT sensor data and AI-driven diagnostics, we aim to reduce accidents caused by technical glitches and optimize fuel efficiency.

Our system integrates 9 distinct sensor inputs—ranging from thermal signatures to knock frequency—to build a multi-dimensional health report of your vehicle. With Twilio-powered emergency alerts, help is always just an SMS away.
